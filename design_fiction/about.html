In the fictional city of Pinedale, a group of scientists have created a prototype for a new virtual reality technology designed for psychotherapy titled the Pinedale Extrinsic Therapeutic Alternate Reality (AKA PETAR). PETAR is meant for those who are physically disabled to live an alternate life through Virtual Reality. However, this technology still is far from perfect and has many flaws.
