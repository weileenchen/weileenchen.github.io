implicit bias:

reflection

implicit
ingrained
instinctual
unchallenged
unseen
infirm bedrock
a lifetime working the explicit
the chosen
the noble
my defended positions
swept out to sea
undertows catch the foundations
sappers mine under the tunnels implied

I'll do what I can to expose my unknowns
my traitorous tendencies
attitudes laid down in me as a child

I've no guarantee to like what's exposed
not to gag on the stench of long covered wounds

but this is more honest
this witnessing to my defaults
this making me see
this ain't saying I'm willing to give in
ain't saying that I agree

if implicit is the foundation
my past-land ground
I choose to soar

–– Linda Mace-Michalik
